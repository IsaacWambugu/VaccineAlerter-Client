## V-alerter for Android 
V-alerter is an alert app whose primary purpose is to notify infant's guardians of the following:
1. List of infants under the guardian's supervision.
1. When an infant's vaccine is due.
1. The days left before a particular vaccine is to be administered.
1. The days past the vaccination date

The app checks for vaccine updates everyday at 9:30 Am local time
